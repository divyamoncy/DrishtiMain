    LoRa

    When the world moves quickly onto 5G and Internet of Things, let's make our foray into a brand new, low-cost, low-power technology that is rewriting the story of electronic communication all over the world.
    LoRa is a brand new communication protocol that introduced extremely low power, low cost modules for communication over distances of 15 to 20 km. Perfect for all designs involving Internet of Things and distributed node networks, the methods and device designs of LoRa is a closely guarded secret, accessible only to very few people working within its niche industry.

    Register now, as a team of five, to be a part of this two day workshop, where you will gain an insight in LoRa communications, and build our own system using a LoRa supported, microcontroller system.
