Lecture Series and Panel Discussion

Want to go deeper into the essence of things happening around you? Want to have your voice heard across platforms? Want to be well equipped in facing an unprecedented danger?

The Conclave provides you with a golden opportunity to attend talks, lectures and panel discussion by prominent activists , reformists and professionals in the area of disaster management and calamity control.

It is necessary that one be educated on tackling a situation in which they have to make ample use of the limited resources they have. The lectures will prove useful in such a situation as the flood wherein people sought out alternate methods of survival. Be educated also regarding how one can do his little to contribute to the revival of the flood hit economy.

It is never more satisfying than to get to know what clearly happened from those who witnessed the events firsthand. We provide you with talks of prominent personalities outlining their experiences and difficulties they faced while handling such a volatile situation such as the floods in Kerala.

Wrapping things up, be a part of the panel discussion comprising of leading activists, reformists and other prominent personnel in the field of dam management, disaster management, crisis facing and planning. Get to understand how and why things are done at what situations.
