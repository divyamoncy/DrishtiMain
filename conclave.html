“When we are rebuilding a house, we are rebuilding a home. When we recover from disaster, we are rebuilding lives and livelihoods."

Drishti ‘18 celebrates “Engineers for Society” - people who can have a definite impact on the lives of those unfortunate few who have fallen victim to nature’s wrath in all its magnitude. Rebuilding doesn’t always benefit just from physical strength but rather from the robustness of the mind too.

We, at The Conclave, provide you with a brilliant opportunity to tap into that same factor. It is an opportunity to reward all with a clear thinking in the time of crisis so as to help solve the aftermaths of the same. In this critical time we pave a pathway for you to step out of the comforts of your home and aid those who lost theirs, those who are undergoing great perils. It is time for you to understand deeply the basis and technicalities behind disaster management and crisis control.

Let your mind create magic for two days. Let it expand beyond conventional and traditions, let it create something sensational, something impactful , something meaningful to those millions who are in dire need of it.

Idea Pitching Competition in association with Kerala Startup Mission

Rules

1. Startup ideas and projects related to disaster management only are invited for registration.

2. A team can have maximum 4 members. Individual participation is allowed

3. Presentation time is restricted to 10 minutes.

4. Mere plagiarism will be disqualified and might face judicial enquiry if copied from an already patented idea.

Rewards

1. Top 10 winning ideas will be selected directly to the next Idea Day of KSUM. Once vetted by experts, these contestants will have opportunity to get an idea grant of upto Rs 2 lakh each to convert into a prototype. If there is a working prototype already, they can get upto 7 lakhs to convert it into a product.

2. Top 3 teams(2 members each) get incubation in KSUM space at Trivandrum, Kochi or Kozhikode (as per their choice) for a period of 6 months free of cost.

3. Top 5 teams get free entry to Fablab or Future lab at Kochi or Trivandrum for a period of 6 months, for converting their idea into prototype. They also get free access to design software available in these labs during this period.

4. If any of the ideas need support for patenting, a patent support upto Rs 2 lakhs will be available.



Lecture Series and Panel Discussion

Want to go deeper into the essence of things happening around you? Want to have your voice heard across platforms? Want to be well equipped in facing an unprecedented danger?

The Conclave provides you with a golden opportunity to attend talks, lectures and panel discussion by prominent activists , reformists and professionals in the area of disaster management and calamity control.

It is necessary that one be educated on tackling a situation in which they have to make ample use of the limited resources they have. The lectures will prove useful in such a situation as the flood wherein people sought out alternate methods of survival. Be educated also regarding how one can do his little to contribute to the revival of the flood hit economy.

It is never more satisfying than to get to know what clearly happened from those who witnessed the events firsthand. We provide you with talks of prominent personalities outlining their experiences and difficulties they faced while handling such a volatile situation such as the floods in Kerala.

Wrapping things up, be a part of the panel discussion comprising of leading activists, reformists and other prominent personnel in the field of dam management, disaster management, crisis facing and planning. Get to understand how and why things are done at what situations.
