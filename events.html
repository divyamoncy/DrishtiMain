HackSquad

Ever dreamt of leading a team to fight against agents of devastation? Do you have what it takes to crack the code and bolt a nut when nature rears its ugly head against humanity? Assemble a team of specialists to mitigate the disaster. Time is not your ally. Work smart and be the ultimate HACKSQUAD.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

The competition is a two day Do It Yourself Challenge consisting of 3 stages. Each stage will act as a filter, with only the best qualifying.
The EVENT will incorporate all the broad engineering fields.
The competing teams must solve aptitude questions, complete different tasks, and finally, make a prototype from scratch so as to solve a relevant problem faced by today's society.

Empresario

“If not this, then what? If not you, then who?”
The manager has his eye on the bottom line; the leader has his eye on the horizon. Are you the kind of the person who can keep your eye on both the extremes!! Then.... this is your platform!!
In today’s highly globalised world, finding your space in any market is not very easy. This event is a platform for for you to use your creative skills to make investors invest in your product when you have many competing for the same.
This event will be conducted in two rounds :
Round 1 ( 10 mins)
Every groups will be provided with 5 important industries on the spot, from which they will have to find any one relevant problem affecting a particular industry from those given.

Judging criteria: The relevance of the problem

Round 2 ( 50 mins)
The groups will be provided with 30 mins to find an innovative technical solution to the problem that your group chose and to prepare the advertisement and presentation for round 2.
1: Advertisement:
A 3 minute advertisement could be performed. Different forms of expression like a short skit, slogans, banners or any other innovative form can be used to advertise your product.
2: Presentation:
They will then be provided a duration of 5 mins to present. The presentation should include the name of the product with a tagline.
3:Sales pitch:
The teams would be provided with 7 mins to make an impressive sales pitch and persuade the judges to invest on their product.
4:Q & A section:
The judges will have a Q&A interaction with the teams for a duration of 5 minutes.

Judging Criteria:
1) Analysis of skills of persuasion, presentation and imagination to market a product and persuade potential customers.
2) Marketing skills
3) Market & customer segmentation to which the product is expected to be launched.
4) Marketing Strategies.
5) The performance is judged based on the clarity in thought, attentiveness, presentation and team coordination.
6) A maximum of 20 minutes will be given for round 2. Points will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
7) The presentation will follow a 5 minute Q&A session from the judges and from the audience.
8) The decision taken by the judges would be final.

Prizes worth over 10k to be won.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1) This is a team event.
2) A team can comprise from 2 to 5 members.
3) Students from any institution can take part.
4) Students should bring valid ID cards.
5) Teams should carry their own laptops and pendrives.
6) If any dispute arises, the decision of the judges will be final.
7) Business plans or other details accessed from online sources will lead to immediate disqualification.
8) Use of internet during the event is strictly prohibited
