HackSquad

Ever dreamt of leading a team to fight against agents of devastation? Do you have what it takes to crack the code and bolt a nut when nature rears its ugly head against humanity? Assemble a team of specialists to mitigate the disaster. Time is not your ally. Work smart and be the ultimate HACKSQUAD.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

The competition is a two day Do It Yourself Challenge consisting of 3 stages. Each stage will act as a filter, with only the best qualifying.
The EVENT will incorporate all the broad engineering fields.
The competing teams must solve aptitude questions, complete different tasks, and finally, make a prototype from scratch so as to solve a relevant problem faced by today's society.
The first stage will be an online MCQ/Written screening test, followed by a time trial, scavenger hunt stage and the final prototyping round.
The decision of the coordinators will be final and binding.

HIGHLIGHT

Ultimate two day challenge to discover the best Hacksquad.
The contest is aimed to bring forth feasible and innovative solutions to problems suffered by the society. It strives to give creative, and possibly, life-changing ideas, produced by the brilliant budding minds of our generation, a larger audience. This will encourage them to tackle day to day problems with a different perspective and realise working, feasible solutions.

Jack of all Trades

Want to expand your knowledge beyond the rote learning in class? Here is your chance. Want to venture into some Augmented Reality?
Drishti brings you AR with Arduino, a one day workshop designed to equip you on the basics of Computer Vision and Arduino. This will pave the way for you to venture into Augmented Reality. The topics covered are:
▪Arduino
▪Computer Vision
▪Servo Motors
▪Basics of AR

Teams of 5 may apply. One kit per team will be provided.
Clear your calendars and call your friends. It's time to put on your learning caps!
