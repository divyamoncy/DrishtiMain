    Ever wondered about those automated industries out there , producing millions of products without man power ?

    Does that ever make you curious about how the machines are automated ?
    Then this workshop is for people like you !
    Industrial Automation is the use of control systems, such as computers or robots and information technology for handling different processes and machinery in an industry to replace a human being.

    Don't miss the chance to get hands-on experience in industrial automation powered by BOSCH. See you there !

    ROS

    Robots are one of the very few science fiction elements of the 50's that have come to life.

    Robotics is in its truest sense, the act of tinkering with machines to create art and as we know, the robots are coming, whether we like it or not, and will change our economy in dramatic ways.

    The ROS Workshop conducted by the Electrical Department of CET as part of our technical fest, *Drishti* is the perfect opportunity for you to delve into the world of robotics. *ROS(Robot Operating System)* has got several drivers and tools to put every bit and piece of your robot into place.

    The workshop will include
    1.Guide to installing the software if required.
    2.A detailed explanation and, guidance in understanding the concepts of ROS.
    3.An example bot that implements the functionalities of ROS will be demonstrated.
