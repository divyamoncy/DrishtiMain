GPS Survey

Let’s unite to forge the new methods of surveying through the GPS surveying workshop conducted by LEICA GEO SYSTEMS, world’s largest surveying and engineering Geographic Information Systems (GIS). This Drishti, the civil association brings you with the chance of being part of this. It’s a one day workshop which will be conducted for two batches of 40 members each on 17th and 18th of which one can register for any required date.

GPS survey workshop focuses on:

- Principle of GPS survey
- Lecture session on different types of GPS survey methods
- Introduction to differential GPS s urvey and corrections
- Practical session on establishing GPS coordinate and survey based on benchmark in the field
