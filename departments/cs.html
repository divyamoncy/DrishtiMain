Competitive Coding Workshop

Want to crack a coding interview ? Wish to see yourself at the top of the leaderboard in an online programming contest ? We can give you the perfect start ! CS Department and ProCoders Club CET presents the ‘Competitive Coding Workshop’.

Problem solving is one top skill on demand to any organization. The role of programmers as problem solvers is inevitable. A programmer needs to be good at math, logic, design and implementation skills. A good programmer is like a good artist. Competitive programming is one domain that tests all the skills a programmer needs to have where you compete with many others to code a puzzle down. It is one sport that involves a lot of thought process and creativity.

Through the hands-on workshop we intend to cover some important algorithms and standard problems on multiple domains to strengthen your algorithmic skills. The workshop spans for two days and the topics we cover include :

Arrays and Strings
Recursion
Backtracking
Number Theory
Dynamic Programming
Greedy Techniques
Lists, Trees and Graphs

Participants are expected to bring laptops.
